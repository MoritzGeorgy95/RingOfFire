Game of Fire is a popular card drinking game that requires players to have quick reflexes and a high tolerance for alcohol. The game is played with a deck of cards and at least four players. To start, the deck of cards is shuffled and placed in a circle face down. A cup filled with a shot of alcohol is placed in the center of the circle. Players take turns picking up a card from the deck and following the corresponding rule:

  • Waterfall

    Everyone has to start drinking at the same time. As soon as player 1 stops drinking, player 2 may stop drinking. Player 3 may stop as soon as player 2 stops drinking, and so on.

  • You

    You decide who drinks

  • Me

    Congrats! Drink a shot!

  • Category

    Come up with a category (e.g. Colors). Each player must enumerate one item from the category.

  • Bust a jive

    Player 1 makes a dance move. Player 2 repeats the dance move and adds a second one.

  • Chicks

    All girls drink.

  • Heaven

    Put your hands up! The last player drinks!

  • Mate

    Pick a mate. Your mate must always drink when you drink and the other way around.

  • Thumbmaster

    Choose a thumbmaster

  • Men

    All men drink.

  • Quizmaster

    Choose a quizmaster

  • Never have i ever...

    Say something you never did. Everyone who did it has to drink.

  • Rule

    Make a rule. Everyone needs to drink when he breaks the rule.
